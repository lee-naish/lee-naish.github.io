Against Censorship

Its not just Nobel Peace Prize winners and their supporters in China who are victims of a culture of censorship.

Its whistle-blowers such as Andrew Fraser 1 2 and Andrew Wilke 3 4 , artists such as Bill Henson 5 6 , social comentators such as Richard Neville 7 and all of us 8 . Censorship is used to silence critics of governments (and governments try to hide this fact). It is alive and well in Australia.

The internet makes effective censorship virtually impossible to enforce. There are many web surfing anonymizers 9 eg, anyone can visit http://anonymouse.org/cgi-bin/anon-www.cgi/http://www.naughty.ru (thats one reason why the "black list" is secret).
The bad guys will use encryption, e-mail, file sharing etc. anyway.

Arguing against censorship is not saying we should have absolute freedom of expression. Some expression causes harm and should be minimized. But is there any evidence that (eg) watching the latest zombie movie causes harm?

But what about the children...?

Freedom of expression needs our support more than censorship
